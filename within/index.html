🌊 Why Hanka?

Most quote apps drown you in notifications, slap ads between every swipe, or serve you recycled motivational poster slogans.

Hanka takes the opposite approach: one curated zen quote per day. Read it. Sit with it. Write a short reflection if the mood strikes. Then a gentle commentary unfolds below, offering context without telling you what to think.

No feeds, no streaks, no gamification. Just a quiet moment of stillness in an otherwise noisy day.

✨ Features

📜 Daily Zen Quote
A carefully curated zen proverb or teaching delivered fresh each day. 50 quotes in the current collection, with more on the way.
🖊️ Personal Reflections
Jot down your thoughts on the day's quote. A lightweight journaling space that stays private and on-device.
📖 Gentle Commentary
Swipe to reveal a brief essay that expands on the quote's meaning. Context, not prescription.
📓 Reflections Journal
Browse your past reflections in a scrollable journal. Each entry is dated, so you can revisit how a quote landed weeks or months later.
🎨 Minimalist Design
Clean, warm typography on a soft cream background. No clutter, no distractions. Just words and space.
🚫 No Ads, No Tracking
No analytics, no third-party SDKs, no data collection. Your reflections never leave your device.
📱 iOS + Android (soon)
Native iOS app available now. Android version is on the way.
